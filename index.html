Frequently asked questions

Do you really accept old or torn Bibles?

Yes. We accept old, worn, and torn Bibles as long as they are still usable. If a Bible is extremely damaged, we may repurpose it responsibly.

What kinds of Bibles are most needed?

Any Bible helps. Study Bibles, large print, children’s Bibles, and standard English Bibles are all welcome.

Where in Kenya will they be distributed?

The Bibles will be distributed across Kilifi, Nairobi, and Meru through a network of 500+ interdenominational congregations, coordinated by St. Joseph House of Hope.

How is the financial gift used?

Shipping support covers packing supplies, shipping fees, and distribution logistics. We keep the process simple and transparent.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes—shipping gifts given through Christ Is The Answer Church are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. We can provide a receipt upon request.
